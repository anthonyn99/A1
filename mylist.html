MyList

Your voice-powered shopping & to-do lists.
Talk to it. Tap the mic at the bottom and just say what you need — “two gallons of milk, a dozen eggs, and a laptop from Best Buy.” The mic keeps listening through pauses; tap it again to stop & process.
Or type it. Use the box at the bottom — same thing, no mic needed.
Smart AI. It splits items, catches quantities & brands, writes short descriptions, and routes things to the right store. You can also add/edit/remove by voice or text, and even create stores (“add Walmart and Target”).
Manual control. Open Manual to add stores or items by hand. Tap an item to edit its name, quantity, store and description. Use Select to delete several at once.
Undo / redo. Made a mistake? Say or type “undo” (or “redo”), or use the ↶ ↷ buttons.
Lists, sync & lock. Make as many lists as you like. Everything syncs live across your devices. Lock a profile with a password from the 🔒 button.
